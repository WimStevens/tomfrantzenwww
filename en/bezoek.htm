Group tour booking

The sculpture garden is open to the public from April 15 to October 15.

Guided tours by the sculptor himself are organized on demand for groups of minimum 15 and maximum 25 participants.

Prior booking for group tours via our online form is required. An e-mail of confirmation will be sent to you with the modalities of payment. Booking will be valid after reception of the payment.

Good shoes are recommended.

Duration of the tour : about 2 hours.

Address: Waaienberg 17 - 3080 Duisburg.

Parking: about 20 cars.

Admission price:

  • Adults (25-64 years): €15,00
  • Adults (+65): €10,00
  • Children (under 12 years): €5,00
  • Guide: €100,00

General information

Tom Frantzen’s Sculpture Garden is located in Duisburg ,Tervuren, in the middle of nature. It is a 2 minutes walk from the wonderful Forêt de Soignes, nearly classified as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

To get there you need to take the 830 bus of De Lijn Public Transport, get off at the Eizerstraat bus stop in Duisburg and walk up to Waaienberg 17 where the guide will be waiting for you. We ask you not to ring the door. The garden entry is next to the house.

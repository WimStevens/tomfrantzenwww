Sculpture parc "Waaienberg"

About twenty years ago, Tom Frantzen started to create a fantasy world in the wild.

Tom Frantzen’s Sculpture Garden is located in Duisburg, Tervuren, in the middle of nature. It is a 2 minutes walk from the wonderful Forêt de Soignes, nearly classified as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

To get there you need to take the 830 bus of De Lijn Public Transport, get off at the Eizerstraat bus stop in Duisburg and walk up to Waaienberg 17 where the guide will be waiting for you. The garden entry is next to the house.

You may find more info about the guided tour and the reservation form on this page.
